About Me

Hello! I'm Mohak Vaish, an Electronics and Communication Engineering student at the Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT, Vellore). My primary interests lie in the fields of Digital VLSI Design, Embedded Systems, and the application of AI/ML in hardware.

I thrive on pushing technological boundaries and am adept at problem-solving, circuit design, and system optimization. Through my hands-on projects and roles in competitive robotics teams like Team Orcus and roboVITics, I have gained practical experience in both hardware and software development.

I am proficient with tools and languages such as Verilog, C++, Python, Intel Quartus, and Cadence Virtuoso, and I'm eager to contribute to cutting-edge advancements in the semiconductor and embedded technology sectors.
